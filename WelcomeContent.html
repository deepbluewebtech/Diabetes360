Before your new Total Diabetes Management App is ready to use, a few settings will need to be entered on the next screen which is also the main settings screen for the app.

You will want to tap these items on the settings screen to tell Diabetes 360 how to help you manage your case.

  • Prescribed Insulin
  • Method Used To Calculate Insulin Dose
    (Sliding Scale or Formula)
  • Target Rate, Corrective Factor, and
    Carb Factor given to you by your doctor
    (for formula method)
  • Insulin On Board Factors
  • Exercise Factors
  • Accuracy for rounding insulin units

All settings must be
by the advice of your Physician.

There are other settings you may be interested in such as:
  • Set up a daily schedule with reminders.
  • Reminder to change the site of your pump.
  • Threshold to remind to check ketone levels.
